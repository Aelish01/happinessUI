Gardening Tips & Plant Care Guides

Bringing nature closer to you with premium Fruit, Indoor, and Outdoor Plants.

Indoor Plants

Air-Purifying Plants

Gardening Tips

  • Place in well-lit but indirect sunlight.
  • Keep soil slightly moist at all times.
  • Avoid placing near strong drafts.
  • Rotate pots monthly for even growth.
  • Use organic compost every 2 months.

Plant Care

  • Dust leaves regularly to maintain efficiency.
  • Repot annually for fresh soil.
  • Check for root rot in humid conditions.
  • Prune damaged leaves promptly.
  • Maintain moderate humidity levels.

Advantages

  • Improves indoor air quality.
  • Reduces airborne toxins.
  • Boosts mood and productivity.
  • Acts as natural d√©cor.
  • Low maintenance.

Low-Light Plants

Gardening Tips

  • Select plants adapted to shade.
  • Avoid overwatering.
  • Fertilize sparingly.
  • Use reflective surfaces to increase light.
  • Group plants to share humidity.

Plant Care

  • Keep in consistent low-light spots.
  • Check soil before watering.
  • Rotate plants every few weeks.
  • Trim yellow leaves.
  • Use pest-free soil.

Advantages

  • Perfect for rooms without direct sunlight.
  • Requires minimal attention.
  • Adds greenery to dark corners.
  • Improves oxygen levels indoors.
  • Long lifespan with basic care.

Decorative Indoor Plants

Gardening Tips

  • Match plant size with room size.
  • Use decorative pots with drainage.
  • Arrange plants at varying heights.
  • Mix foliage shapes and colors.
  • Ensure adequate lighting.

Plant Care

  • Clean pots to prevent algae.
  • Water based on plant species.
  • Check for pests regularly.
  • Keep foliage dust-free.
  • Repot when roots outgrow container.

Advantages

  • Enhances home aesthetics.
  • Boosts relaxation and creativity.
  • Complements interior design.
  • Creates focal points in rooms.
  • Variety of styles available.

Outdoor Plants

Gardening Tips

  • Choose plants suited to your climate.
  • Prepare soil with compost before planting.
  • Water deeply and less frequently.
  • Mulch to retain soil moisture.
  • Prune seasonally for shape and health.

Plant Care

  • Protect from extreme weather.
  • Fertilize during growing seasons.
  • Remove weeds regularly.
  • Watch for signs of pests or disease.
  • Stake taller plants for support.

Advantages

  • Improves outdoor aesthetics.
  • Provides shade and shelter.
  • Supports biodiversity.
  • Increases property value.
  • Enhances outdoor living spaces.

Fruit Trees

Gardening Tips

  • Plant in full sun for best yield.
  • Space trees adequately for growth.
  • Water regularly during dry spells.
  • Prune to encourage fruiting.
  • Use organic fertilizers for better taste.

Plant Care

  • Protect young trees from pests.
  • Mulch around the base to retain moisture.
  • Thin fruits to improve size and quality.
  • Check for fungal infections.
  • Harvest at peak ripeness.

Advantages

  • Provides fresh, organic fruits.
  • Reduces grocery costs.
  • Encourages outdoor activity.
  • Improves local ecology.
  • Long-term sustainable food source.
